Augmenting Fusion Data Intelligence with
Manager Hierarchy PVOs

Oracle Fusion Data Intelligence (FDI) provides robust pre-built analytics. However, organizational structures are unique. By leveraging core Human Capital Management (HCM) Public View Objects (PVOs), organizations can extract deep, multi-level manager hierarchies to customize their semantic models and dashboards.

Essential Hierarchy PVOs

Selecting the correct Public View Object is critical. Different PVOs serve different analytical requirements, ranging from primary assignments to broader workforce structural reporting.

AssignmentManagerHierarchyPVO

FscmTopModelAM.HcmTopModelAM.HcmEmploymentTopModelAM.AssignmentManagerHierarchyPVO

The most widely used PVO for augmentation. It extracts the manager hierarchy based specifically on the worker's assignments. Crucial for matrix organizations where employees might have different managers for different roles.

PersonManagerHierarchyPVO

FscmTopModelAM.HcmTopModelAM.HcmPeopleTopModelAM.PersonManagerHierarchyPVO

Extracts the hierarchy at the person level rather than the assignment level. Best used when analyzing headcount, global diversity metrics, or overall corporate structure irrespective of granular daily tasks.

DepartmentHierarchyPVO

FscmTopModelAM.HcmTopModelAM.HcmOrganizationsTopModelAM.DepartmentHierarchyPVO

While not a 'manager' hierarchy per se, this is frequently joined alongside manager PVOs to provide the departmental roll-up structure (e.g., Team -> Department -> Division -> Enterprise).
